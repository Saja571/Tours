About Us

Welcome to Lak Derana Tours, your gateway to the tranquil beauty and holistic wellness of Sri Lanka. Our focus on wellness tourism includes authentic Ayurvedic treatments and relaxation therapies, rooted in centuries-old traditions. Sri Lanka's rich cultural heritage, stunning beaches, lush forests, and vibrant scenery provide the perfect backdrop for your journey. Experience the island's rich culture, including authentic Sri Lankan cuisine infused with aromatic spices, and discover the timeless wisdom of traditional Ayurvedic practices.
